Disclaimer

This Universal Unit Converter provides conversions based on widely accepted standard conversion factors. It is intended for general engineering calculations and estimations.

Important Considerations:

  • Significant Figures: Results are rounded for display; maintain appropriate significant figures for critical applications.
  • Density/Specific Gravity: Flow and volume conversions do not account for fluid density, which is critical for mass flow rate calculations.
  • Absolute vs. Gauge Pressure: Ensure consistency (both absolute or both gauge) when dealing with pressure units.
  • Temperature Scales: Be mindful of absolute vs. relative temperature scales (Kelvin/Rankine vs. Celsius/Fahrenheit).
  • Specific Industry Standards: While generally compliant, specific industry or company standards may require particular unit usages or conversion factors. Always refer to relevant industry standards (e.g., ASME, ASTM, ISO, API) for critical design and operational applications.

For highly critical applications, always cross-verify results with official engineering handbooks, specific industry standards, and qualified professionals.
