Instrumentation Tips

Process measurement, control system design, and sensor optimization techniques

Why This Matters

Control valve sizing directly impacts process safety, energy efficiency, and equipment reliability. Improper valve selection causes flashing, cavitation, and instability in critical control loops. Understanding flow coefficient (Cv) calculations and pressure drop considerations enables engineers to select valves that provide precise control while protecting equipment from damage, potentially preventing catastrophic failures in safety-critical applications.
