Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this site unique compared to other online calculators?

Our primary differentiator is transparency. While other tools give you a final answer, we provide a complete, step-by-step breakdown of the entire calculation. This educational approach allows you to verify the results, understand the methodology, and deepen your own engineering knowledge. It's a tool built for professionals who value accuracy and learning.

How do I know the calculations are accurate and reliable?

Accuracy is our foundation. Every formula is meticulously researched and implemented based on the latest versions of globally recognized standards (IEC, IEEE, etc.). By showing you every step of the calculation, we provide an unparalleled level of transparency, allowing you to cross-reference our methodology with the standards themselves.

Are these calculators really free to use?

Yes, absolutely. All calculators and tools on our platform are 100% free. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality engineering resources to the global community. The site is supported by non-intrusive advertising to cover development and maintenance costs.

Is this website mobile-friendly?

Yes. The entire website, including all calculators, is fully responsive. It is designed to work seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones, so you can perform critical calculations whether you are in the office or on a project site.

Can I use these results for official project submissions?

Our tools are an extremely powerful aid for design, verification, and cross-checking. However, they must not be used as a sole substitute for professional engineering judgment. All results must be reviewed and approved by a qualified engineer responsible for the project, ensuring compliance with all local codes and specific requirements.

How often are new calculators added?

We are constantly in a state of development. Our goal is to regularly release new tools and update existing ones to reflect the latest standard revisions. You can follow our blog for announcements on new calculator releases.

Do you store any of my input data?

No. Your privacy is critical. All calculations are performed in your browser. We do not store, view, or collect any of the specific parameters or data you input into the calculators. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details on general site usage data.
