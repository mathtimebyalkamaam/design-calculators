Material & Chemical Resistance Chart

This chart provides general guidance on the chemical resistance and compatibility of common engineering materials across various chemical classes. It's a vital reference for selecting materials for piping, vessels, seals, and other components in industrial applications.

Compatibility Ratings Key:

  • Excellent: Suitable for continuous service; very little or no effect.
  • Good: Minor to moderate effects, may be suitable depending on specific conditions (e.g., temperature, concentration, duration). Test before use.
  • Fair: Moderate to severe effects, generally not recommended for continuous service. Very limited use.
  • Poor: Not recommended for any service; severe corrosive effects.
  • N/A: Information not readily available or application typically uncommon.

Disclaimer: This chart provides general guidelines. Compatibility can be highly dependent on specific chemical concentration, temperature, pressure, exposure duration, presence of impurities, and mechanical stress. Always consult specific material data sheets, manufacturer recommendations, and perform thorough testing for critical applications.
