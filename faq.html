Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Design Calculators?

Design Calculators is a comprehensive online platform providing a suite of engineering calculators and tools for electrical, mechanical, and instrumentation professionals, students, and enthusiasts. My mission is to simplify complex computations and enhance productivity.

What standards are your calculators based on?

My calculators are meticulously engineered and rigorously validated based on recognized industry standards such as IEC, IEEE, NEC, ASME, API, TEMA, ANSI, IEC/ISO, ISHRAE, NAMUR, ISA, and NFPA/OSHA. I strive to ensure accuracy and reliability in every calculation.

Are the calculators free to use?

Yes, all calculators and tools on Design Calculators are completely free to use. My goal is to provide accessible and reliable resources to the engineering community.

Can I use these calculations for professional projects?

While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, these tools should not be used as a substitute for professional engineering judgment. Always verify calculation results against applicable industry standards, local codes, and specific project requirements. Please refer to our Terms of Service and Disclaimer for more information.

How often are new calculators added?

I am continuously working to expand this collection of tools and resources to meet the evolving needs of the engineering community. New calculators and features are added regularly, so check back often!

How can I suggest a new calculator or feature?

I welcome your feedback and suggestions! While I don't currently have a direct contact form, you can reach out to us through our email id info.onesharma@gmail.com, or keep an eye out for a dedicated feedback mechanism in the future.

Is there a dark mode available?

Yes, Design Calculators features a dark mode for a more comfortable viewing experience, especially in low-light conditions. You can toggle between light and dark mode using the switch located in the header of the website.
