Frequently Asked Questions

What is Design Calculators?

Design Calculators is a comprehensive online platform providing a suite of engineering calculators and tools for electrical, mechanical, and instrumentation professionals, students, and enthusiasts. Our mission is to simplify complex computations and enhance productivity.

What standards are your calculators based on?

These calculators are meticulously engineered and rigorously validated based on recognized industry standards such as IEC, IEEE, NEC, ASME, API, TEMA, and ISA. We strive to ensure accuracy and reliability in every calculation.

Are the calculators free to use?

Yes, all calculators and tools on Design Calculators are completely free to use. Our goal is to provide accessible and reliable resources to the engineering community.

Can I use these calculations for professional projects?

While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, these tools should not be used as a substitute for professional engineering judgment. Always verify calculation results against applicable industry standards, local codes, and specific project requirements. Please refer to our Terms of Service for more information.

How often are new calculators added?

We are continuously working to expand our collection of tools. New calculators and features are added regularly, so check back often!

How can I suggest a new calculator or feature?

We welcome your feedback and suggestions! You can reach out to us through our Contact Page with any ideas you have.

Is there a dark mode available?

Yes, Design Calculators features a dark mode for a more comfortable viewing experience. You can toggle between light and dark mode using the switch located in the header of the website.
