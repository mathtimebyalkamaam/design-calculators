Advanced Cable Sizing Calculator (AC/DC)

This calculator determines the appropriate cable size for AC or DC systems based on load current, distance, and voltage drop, adhering to international standards like IEC 60364.

It considers factors such as ambient temperature, detailed installation methods, soil thermal resistivity, load type, and performs short-circuit withstand checks for a comprehensive, standards-compliant sizing solution.
